Project Overview

WanderLust 2.0 is a dynamic and interactive travel listing platform designed to provide users with a seamless experience for discovering, reviewing, and booking travel destinations. The platform offers a range of features including travel listings, user reviews, personalized recommendations, and map-based navigation for better exploration. By integrating real-time map functionality, users can easily search for destinations and get detailed information, all while benefiting from personalized content and recommendations.

The key objective is to simplify travel planning for users by leveraging advanced filtering options, review systems, and interactive maps. Future expansions may include a booking system for trips and accommodations, enhancing the platform’s usability for a complete travel planning experience.
